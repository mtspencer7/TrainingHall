Privacy Policy for Training Hall Browser Extension Last updated: 02/25/2026 This Privacy Policy explains how the Training Hall browser extension (“Training Hall”, “the extension”) collects, uses, and protects information. 1. Information We Collect Training Hall collects limited, non-personal usage data to improve the functionality and reliability of the extension. This may include: Feature usage (e.g., which tutorials or buttons are clicked) Basic interaction events related to the extension experience Anonymous performance and error metrics Training Hall does NOT collect: Personal identifying information Passwords Form inputs Keystrokes Content from web pages outside what is necessary to display tutorials Financial, health, or sensitive personal data 2. How We Use Information The information collected is used only to: Deliver relevant tutorial guidance Improve extension functionality and usability Monitor performance and diagnose issues Understand feature adoption at an aggregate level All analytics data is collected in an aggregated and anonymized form. 3. Data Sharing & Third Parties Training Hall does not sell or share personal data. The extension uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage and performance metrics. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics or any other third party. 4. Data Storage Training Hall stores limited state and preferences locally in your browser (e.g., tutorial progress, settings). No personal information is stored on external servers. 5. Security We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the extension. The extension only accesses the minimum data required to provide in-context tutorials. 6. Your Choices You may disable or remove the extension at any time using your browser’s extension management tools. You may also disable analytics features through extension settings (if available). 7. Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected with a revised “Last updated” date. 8.
